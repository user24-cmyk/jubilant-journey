What is Arcade?

Arcade is a one-of-a-kind playable sampler and instrument plugin with new content added weekly. It helps users find inspiration, add flavor and beat writer's block.


You can easily modify all sounds directly in Arcade to make them your own. An Arcade subscription starts at only $14.99/mo USD and includes access to 50+ product lines, 4,300+ samples, 1,200+ chromatic instruments, and 62,000+ samples. Always royalty free.

Can I record in Arcade?

While Arcade is available as a standalone application where you can explore and play sounds, you will need a Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) in order to record, arrange and share those sounds.

+

Which DAWs does Arcade work with?

Arcade is supported by all major DAWs in 64-bit VST, VST3, AU and AAX format. This includes but is not limited to: GarageBand, FL Studio, Ableton Live, Logic Pro X, ProTools, Studio One, Cubase, Reaper, Maschine, Reason, and Bitwig.
